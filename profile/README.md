# Mumbai University Question Papers - Free Archive

> Reclaiming Mumbai University's exam papers from a paywall

## The Situation

Mumbai University created 2,400+ question papers. Due to MU's website rework, these papers are temporarily unavailable on their official site.

**munotes.in exploited this gap**: They scraped these papers and are now selling them without permission: ₹49 per paper, ₹149 per bundle.

Students pay for materials their own university created (that they can't find on MU's official site).

## What We Did

We extracted and organized all these papers. **They're now free.**
